Your Guide to Craft Brew in Kalamazoo

In Kalamazoo, Michigan we aren’t just beer lovers—we’re beer fanatics. Whether you’re a hophead or stout-slammer, we have sensational craft beers and top-shelf venues guaranteed to tickle your fancy upper-lip-hair.

The taps around town vary more often than Midwestern seasons so gulp down all the unique styles and flavors. Over half of the breweries are within walking distance of one another so you’re always close to your next pint.

Since our city’s first, and Michigan’s oldest, commercial brewery opened in 1837, our passion for craft beers has infected the globe. This past April, U.S World and News Report named Kalamazoo as on of the 8 undervalued beer cities in the world In 2013, Kalamazoo was named the #3 Beer Town in America by Business Insider and placed 2nd in the Beer City USA contest poll ran by Examiner.com. We also welcomed 6 new breweries to our town that same year.

Brewery Tours & Pub Crawls

If you want to a wider sampling of the quality brew in the Zoo, plan a brewery tour or pub crawl. Sample beer at several dowtown breweries and meet local beer producers on a three-to-four-hour walking tour (don't worry, over half the breweries in Kalamazoo are just a hop away from one another.)

Tours start June 7 and take place every Saturday through summer. Cost is $19 per person.

Kalamazoo Beer Week

Join us in January as well for Kalamazoo Beer Week: a weeklong celebration of craft beer and the community that loves it. During this beerapalooza, sample Kalamazoo’s local brews with special tastings, dinners, pub crawls and other events.
