About Arcadia Ales

Battle Creek’s Arcadia Ales recently joined the Kalamazoo brew scene, opening a brewery, pub and kitchen along the Kalamazoo River in May 2014. The entire opening day was Arcadia’s first “Drink One Down – Support Your Town” event: for every beer sold during their official opening, the brewery made a matching donation to Kalamazoo’s Big Brothers Big Sisters. The site is already a part of Kalamazoo’s history: floor joists from the first city hall visible in the brewery’s floors, along the bar trim and on drink rails. Arcadia owner Tim Surprise estimates the wood is over 250 years old.

The bright, airy space includes approximately 400 feet of tranquil riverfront property. Arcadia plans to offer an exciting outdoor experience in addition to the existing patio. Construction will continue throughout the summer on a beer garden that will include an outdoor music venue with a stage and bandshell large enough for a full orchestra, a disc golf putting green, a bocce ball court, and a canoe and kayak lunch area.

The Brew

Arcadia offers 17 beers at their brewery: a variety of their classic, speciality, seasonal and reserve beers as well as a few exclusives. Some popular choices include:

  • Sky High Rye
  • Whitsun Ale
  • Loch Down Scotch Ale
  • Thunder Trail ESB
  • B-Craft Black IPA
