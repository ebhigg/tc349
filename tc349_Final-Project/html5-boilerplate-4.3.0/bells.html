About Bell's Brewery

Larry Bell started the state's oldest microbrewery in 1985. Since then, it has grown into a regional craft brewery that was named 2014 Top Brewer in Michigan and 5th Best Brewer in the World in Ratebeer.com's Best Awards. Bell's operates in over 20 states and distributes over 20 of their beers.

Bell's Eccentric Café

Bell's Brewery, Inc. became the first Michigan brewery to serve beer by the glass to the public in June 1993. Beer lovers can taste many of Bell's well-know beers as well as smaller batch brews that are available only at their pub, the Eccentric Café

Vistors enjoy the venue's warm atmosphere and spacious beer garden. In addition, the collection of art and emphemera, and live music, always give visitors something to talk about.

Bell's General Store

Beer is available to-go next door at Bell's General Store. If you're extra thirsty, fill up at their Growler Station. They also offer homebrewing supplies, wearables and other memorabilia.

Tours

Free public tours of their original brewery, connected to the renovated Eccentric Café, are offered every weekend. Tours last apprximately 30–45 minutes and begin at the following times:

  • Saturdays 12:30pm, 1:30pm, 2:30pm and 3:30pm
  • Sundays 1:30pm, 2:30pm, and 3:30pm

The Brew

At any given time, Bell's has over 20 beers on tap. Many are brewed right next door and are exclusive to the Eccentric Café. If you want to ponder your first drink before you arrive, check out their Tap Cam to see what they're pouring that day.

Bell's offers some of the best beers you'll ever drink so try as many as you can. Several ranked in Ratebeer.com's 11th annual Best Awards:

  • Two Hearted Ale named top India Pale Ale in the world
  • Hopslam named #4 Double India Pale Ale in the world
  • Blacknote Stout named #4 Stout in the world
  • Bell's Expedition Stout named #14 Stout in the world

  • Kalamazoo Stout
