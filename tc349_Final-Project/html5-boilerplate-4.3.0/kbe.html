About Kalamazooo Beer Exchange

The Kalamazoo Beer Exchange (KBE) is a two-story multi-tap bar and grill located in the restored Globe Building downtown. While they don’t brew their own, KBE offers 28 rotating draught beers from around the world.

What makes 2014's Best Bar in Michigan such an exciting experience is that they base prices of their draught beers on real-time sales, like the stock market. When the Beer Market opening bell rings at 6pm, the real fun begins. For the rest of the night “market crashes” are imminent, during which all drafts drop to ridiculous prices.

It becomes an ever-changing happy hour and each visit differs from the last. Crazy things happen and energy flows through the room as people rush for a new glass when the market crashes (we’ve seen an aromatic IPA cost nearly as little as a Bud Light, seriously.)

The Kalamazoo Beer Exchange also offers a unique menu of reimagined traditional pub dishes. Their open kitchen design allows visitors to see the hands that passionalty prepare mouth-watering bar staples with a twist.

The Brew

With 28 beers on tap, every beer palette will be satisfied. Check out their menu to see everything that's on tap now. Some current favorites include:

  • Huma Lupa Licious Short's Brewing Company, Bellaire, MI
  • Enjoy By 7. 04. 14 Stone Brewing Company, Escondido, CA
