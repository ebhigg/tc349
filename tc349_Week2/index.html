Who I Am

I’m Eric Baxter Higgins. Grew up in Portage, MI. Finishing my BA in Advertising and specialization in design this summer.

Copywriting is my passion. I love the impact of thoughtful headlines, and themelines. I like the wit, humor, sarcasm, etc. of the conversational writing style. You might notice that I write with lots of en dashes – in my mind it’s the best punctuation character in written language (nerdy yes, but I don’t care.)

I like graphic design and web design as well, and seek to continuously improve my knowledge of these subjects. I’m constantly coming up with new ideas across different media, and I understand the synergy of writing copy and thinking visually when developing anything from a print advertisement to a multi-page website.

My Hardware

I mainly work on my 15” MacBook Pro. I’ve had it for about 3 years now and haven’t had a single problem with it.

I’m considering adding a second monitor for more work space – and so I don’t have to constantly flip through applications to find the perfect song for my work mood or watch something ridiculous

I use my iPhone 4S mainly for self-organization, photography, social media, and sports. I have serious appreciation for apps with intuitive user interfaces.

My Software

I just upgraded to OS X Maverick – don’t know why I waited until now, but am starting to explore what’s new with it. Can’t see myself ever going back to a PC, I love the software’s simplicity.

I used Dreamweaver in my previous experience writing HMTL5 and CSS code. I downloaded TextMate to use for a text editor, so I’ll see how I like it (this page is my first time using it).

I store my photography in iPhoto since it conveniently links up with an iPhone’s photo stream. From there I pull photos that I consider using in a specific project into Photoshop to play around with them. I also keep tons of photos for inspiration – cool typography, art styles, ad campaign executions, anything to serve as brain fuel for my own concepts and pieces.

I listen to all my music on Spotify. I like the ease of sharing new music with a few of my buddies. Lately I’ve been going between classic rock and electronic – an odd combination I know. I use iTunes to occasionally purhcase a song that I can’t live without.

My Dream Setup

While I don’t own a tablet, I dream of being able to carry around an all-in-one 8.5” x 11” tablet – one that runs and edits any type of file from a one-page word document to a multi-page Illustrator file.

It’d have a wireless mouse as well as a pen stylus for drawing or taking handwritten notes – for some things I like handwritten notes, while for others I find a Word notebook doc more convenient.

And an external hard drive with over a terabyte of storage and automatic VCS functionality. I hate the process of transferring files between my flash drive and multiple computers. After working on another computer, I sometimes forget to transfer updates back to my computer – I suppose I’m at fault for not always renaming new file versions, but it’d be great not having to think about that.
